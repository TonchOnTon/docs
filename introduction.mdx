tonch is a Pump.fun-style launchpad for TON Jettons. Anyone can deploy a token in three clicks — fill a form, sign with a TON wallet, your token opens on a transparent bonding curve. When the curve fills (1,000 TON collected), liquidity migrates to a DeDust v2 pool and the LP tokens are forwarded to a write-only locker, sealing the liquidity permanently.

There are no admin pause opcodes, no creator allocations, and no special early-buyer pricing. Every buy and sell — including the deployer's optional dev-buy — goes through the same curve at the same fee.

## What you'll need

- A TON wallet that supports TonConnect (Tonkeeper, MyTonWallet, OpenMask, Tonhub).
- TON in your wallet — at least 0.5 TON for trading, 4 TON for launching (3 TON fee + ~1 TON gas).
- A square image URL to use as your token's avatar (only required for launching).

The app lives at [tonch.app](https://tonch.app). All on-chain state is sourced from the contracts themselves — the indexer and frontend are conveniences, not the source of truth for funds.
